## Overview

This repository contains an open-source Computer Science & Engineering curriculum designed to educate engineers with rigorous foundations in mathematics, computer science, algorithms, and artificial intelligence.

Unlike conventional curricula that emphasize rapidly evolving technologies, this curriculum focuses on enduring principles that enable learners to understand, design, and build intelligent computational systems from first principles.

The curriculum is modular, implementation-oriented, and suitable for both formal academic programs and self-directed learners.


---

## Curriculum Philosophy

The curriculum is guided by six foundational principles:

* **Mathematics as the Language of Computing**
* **Algorithmic Thinking and Computational Problem Solving**
* **First-Principles Computer Science**
* **Artificial Intelligence as the Culmination of Computer Science**
* **Open Learning and Progressive Mastery**
* **Academic Flexibility and Interdisciplinary Growth**

Rather than relying on existing frameworks or software abstractions, students progressively implement algorithms, data structures, computer systems, and machine learning models to develop a deep understanding of modern computing.

---

## Graduate Profile

Graduates of this curriculum will develop:

* Strong mathematical and analytical reasoning
* Algorithmic problem-solving skills
* Fundamental knowledge of computer systems
* First-principles understanding of Artificial Intelligence
* Practical software engineering experience through substantial projects
* Open-source development practices
* Independent learning habits for continuous growth

## Vision

The long-term objective of this curriculum is to build engineers whose expertise is rooted in mathematics, algorithms, computer science, and first-principles reasoning rather than transient technologies.

Through rigorous theory, systematic implementation, and substantial engineering projects, learners develop the knowledge required to design scalable software systems, contribute to open-source, and build modern intelligent systems.
